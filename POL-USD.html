Technical Indicators

Several technical indicators are widely used in crypto trading, which can be classified into trend, momentum, volume, and volatility indicators. Some of the most popular indicators include Moving Averages, Relative Strength Index (RSI), MACD, Bollinger Bands, Fibonacci Retracement, and Stochastic Oscillator.

  • Moving Averages: These help to smooth out price action by filtering out noise and highlighting the overall trend.
  • Relative Strength Index (RSI): This is a momentum indicator that measures the speed and change of price movements to determine whether an asset is overbought (above 70) or oversold (below 30).
  • Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD): MACD is a trend and momentum analysis tool that is calculated using two Exponential Moving Averages (EMAs).
  • Bollinger Bands: Bollinger Bands are lines that wrap around the outer boundaries of price action, and can be used as part of mean-reversion strategy.
