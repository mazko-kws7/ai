To calculate RSI, we first need to calculate the Average Gain and Average Loss over a period (e.g., 14 days).

  1. Calculate Daily Change: Subtract the previous day’s closing price from the current day’s closing price.
  2. Separate Gains and Losses: If the result is positive, it’s a gain. If it’s negative, it’s a loss.
  3. Calculate Average Gain and Average Loss: Over the past 14 days.
  4. Calculate Relative Strength (RS): RS = Average Gain / Average Loss
  5. Calculate RSI: RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))
