Background Information on PEOPLE-USD

PEOPLE represents the token of ConstitutionDAO, a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) that was formed to crowdfund the purchase of a copy of the U.S. Constitution at a Sotheby’s auction in November 2021. Although the DAO raised over $40 million in ETH, they were outbid. Afterward, ConstitutionDAO shut down and offered refunds to contributors. The PEOPLE token was initially intended to give holders voting rights over where the Constitution would be placed if the DAO had won the auction.
