RSI Calculation

  1. Calculate price changes: Find the difference between each day’s closing price and the previous day’s.
  2. Separate gains and losses: List the gains (positive changes) and losses (negative changes).
  3. Average gains and losses (14-day period): Sum the gains over 14 days and divide by 14. Do the same for losses (using absolute values).
  4. Calculate Relative Strength (RS): RS = Average Gain / Average Loss
  5. Calculate RSI: RSI = 100 - [100 / (1 + RS)]
