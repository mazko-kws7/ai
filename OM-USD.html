    • 20-day MA: Sum the closing prices of the last 20 days and divide by 20.

  • Relative Strength Index (RSI):
    • RSI = 100 - [100 / (1 + RS)]
    • RS = Average gain of up periods / Average loss of down periods (over a period, typically 14 days)
    • Interpretation: RSI values above 70 are often considered overbought (potential sell), and values below 30 are considered oversold (potential buy).
  • Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD):
    • MACD Line: 12-day EMA (Exponential Moving Average) - 26-day EMA
    • Signal Line: 9-day EMA of the MACD Line
    • Interpretation: When the MACD line crosses above the Signal Line, its a bullish signal (potential buy). When the MACD line crosses below the Signal Line, its a bearish signal (potential sell).
