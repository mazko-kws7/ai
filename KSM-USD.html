Technical Analysis

Technical indicators are tools that help traders make informed decisions by summarizing factors such as price, volume, trend, and momentum. Common indicators include moving averages, Relative Strength Index (RSI), MACD, and Bollinger Bands.

  • Moving Averages (MA): Used for trend analysis, with rising MAs indicating an uptrend.
  • Relative Strength Index (RSI): Measures the speed and change of price movements, with values below 30 indicating oversold conditions and above 70 indicating overbought conditions.
  • MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence): Combines trend and momentum analysis, calculated using two Exponential Moving Averages (EMAs).
  • Bollinger Bands: Use standard deviation to determine the price trend and volatility.
