Rationale for Volume Confirmation:

Volume is a crucial indicator of the strength and validity of a price trend. A price breakout (either upward or downward) that is not accompanied by a significant increase in volume is often considered a false breakout. This means the price may briefly move outside the established range, but the move is not supported by strong buying or selling pressure and is likely to reverse.

Heres why volume confirmation is important:

  1. Indicates Conviction: High volume during a breakout suggests that a large number of traders are participating and believe in the new direction of the price. This conviction increases the likelihood that the breakout will sustain itself.
  2. Reduces False Signals: Without volume confirmation, a price breakout could simply be due to random market fluctuations or manipulation. Volume acts as a filter to reduce these false signals.
  3. Provides Liquidity: High volume implies there are enough buyers and sellers to facilitate the price movement. This liquidity helps to ensure that the trend can continue without being easily reversed.

Therefore, any trading strategy should incorporate volume analysis to confirm the strength and sustainability of potential breakouts or trend reversals.
