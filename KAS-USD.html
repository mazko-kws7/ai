14-day RSI (Relative Strength Index): RSI helps indicate overbought or oversold conditions. Heres how Ill approximate it:

  1. Calculate price changes: Subtract each days closing price from the previous days.
  2. Separate gains and losses: Create two lists, one for days with gains and one for days with losses.
  3. Calculate average gain and average loss: Average the gains over the past 14 days and the losses over the past 14 days (using absolute values).
  4. Calculate the Relative Strength (RS): RS = Average Gain / Average Loss
  5. Calculate RSI: RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))
