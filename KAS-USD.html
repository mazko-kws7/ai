Overview of Kaspa (KAS)

Kaspa (KAS) is a proof-of-work (PoW) cryptocurrency that implements the GHOSTDAG protocol. Unlike traditional blockchains, GHOSTDAG does not orphan blocks created in parallel, allowing them to coexist and be ordered in consensus. Kaspa is designed to be faster than Bitcoin, with the ability to generate and confirm multiple blocks per second.
