II. Technical Indicator Considerations

  • MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence): MACD combines trend and momentum analysis. It uses two EMAs and can signal potential buy or sell opportunities when the MACD line crosses above or below the signal line.
  • Bollinger Bands: Bollinger Bands measure volatility. When the price touches or breaks the upper band, it may indicate an overbought condition; when it touches or breaks the lower band, it may indicate an oversold condition.
