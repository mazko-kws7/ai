Overview of Ethena (ENA)

Ethena is a synthetic dollar protocol built on Ethereum. It aims to provide a crypto-native alternative to traditional stablecoins, not reliant on traditional banking infrastructure. Its synthetic dollar, USDe, is referred to as the “Internet Bond” and offers a dollar-denominated savings instrument with DeFi benefits. Ethena uses crypto assets like ETH and BTC as collateral, combined with derivatives positions to maintain stability. It employs a “delta hedging” process where derivatives positions adjust automatically to offset crypto collateral value changes.
