Calculating 14-day RSI

  1. Calculate Average Gains and Losses:
    • First, calculate the difference between each day’s closing price and the previous day’s.
    • Separate gains (positive differences) and losses (negative differences).
    • Calculate the average gain and average loss over the past 14 days.
  2. Calculate Relative Strength (RS):
    • RS = Average Gain / Average Loss
  3. Calculate RSI:
    • RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + RS))

Given the sample data, let’s assume after calculating the average gains and losses over 14 days, we get:

  • Average Gain ≈ 0.1
  • Average Loss ≈ 0.2

Therefore:

  • RS = 0.1 / 0.2 = 0.5
  • RSI = 100 - (100 / (1 + 0.5)) = 100 - (100 / 1.5) ≈ 33.33

Calculating MACD

    • Calculate the Exponential Moving Averages (EMAs) for 12 days and 26 days.
  2. Calculate MACD Line:
    • MACD Line = 12-day EMA - 26-day EMA
  3. Calculate 9-day EMA of MACD Line (Signal Line):
    • Calculate the 9-day EMA of the MACD line.
  4. Calculate MACD Histogram:
    • MACD Histogram = MACD Line - Signal Line
