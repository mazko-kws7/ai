Overview of LEO-USD

UNUS SED LEO (LEO) is the utility token for iFinex, the parent company of the Bitfinex exchange. It was created in May 2019 to address a financial challenge when funds handled by Crypto Capital were confiscated. A total of 1 billion LEO tokens were sold at 1 USDT each during the initial exchange offering (IEO), raising $1 billion. The token is used within the Bitfinex ecosystem, offering reduced trading fees and other incentives. iFinex commits to monthly LEO burns, using at least 27% of its revenue to buy back tokens. This buyback mechanism creates consistent buying pressure, potentially insulating LEO from extreme volatility.
