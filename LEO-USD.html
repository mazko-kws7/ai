Cryptocurrency Overview: LEO-USD

  • LEO Token (UNUS SED LEO): LEO is the utility token for the Bitfinex cryptocurrency exchange. It was launched in May 2019 by iFinex, Bitfinexs parent company, after Crypto Capital, which processed Bitfinexs payments, had funds seized. The token aimed to enhance trading and lending experiences on the Bitfinex platform. LEO provides benefits to holders, such as reduced trading fees on the Bitfinex exchange.
  • Token Burn Mechanism: iFinex is committed to buying back LEO tokens from the market and burning them monthly. The amount repurchased and burned equals at least 27% of iFinexs revenues. This mechanism continues until 100% of the tokens are redeemed.
  • Dual Blockchain Issuance: LEO tokens were issued on both the Ethereum and EOS blockchains.
