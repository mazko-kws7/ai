    • A 50-day MA crossing below the 200-day MA is a bearish signal (Death Cross).

  2. Relative Strength Index (RSI):

    • Calculate the 14-day RSI.
    • Steps:
      1. Calculate price changes: U (Upward change) and D (Downward change).
      2. Calculate average gains (AG) and average losses (AL) over 14 days.
      3. Calculate Relative Strength (RS) = AG / AL
      4. Calculate RSI = 100 - [100 / (1 + RS)]

    Interpretation:

    • RSI above 70 indicates overbought conditions (potential sell signal).
    • RSI below 30 indicates oversold conditions (potential buy signal).
